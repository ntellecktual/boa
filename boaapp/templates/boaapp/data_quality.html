Why This Matters at Omni
Omni Hotels operates 50+ properties across North America. Reservation data flows from Opera PMS into the data warehouse nightly. Revenue management models depend on accurate rates, dates, and loyalty tiers. A single invalid check-out date can cascade into incorrect ADR calculations, broken forecasting models, and misrouted loyalty rewards. This framework catches issues before they reach downstream analytics.
